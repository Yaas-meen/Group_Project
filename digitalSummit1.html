Ihifix Digital Summit

For Educators 2020

Promoting the use of ICTs for driving teaching, learning and research


Aims & Objectives

The aim of this summit is to evolve innovative ways at ensuring the adoption of technologies by educators in ways to accelerate the access to qualitative education by promoting the use of ICTs for driving teaching, learning and research.

How did our schools fair during the covid-19 pandemic in terms of continuity in academic activity, learners engagement and staff productivity?

  • What potentials does technology have that our educators can take advantage of for provisioning effective and qualitative education for learners of all ages?
  • What are the infrastructure state and gaps of our schools for digitally enhanced teaching and learning?
  • What steps should our educators take in bridging the technological deficit for seamless adoption and implementation of technologies for education
